# Music Domain

> Track albums worth revisiting and capture listening notes while impressions are fresh.
> Last updated: YYYY-MM-DD

---

## Purpose

A listening log and review archive. Use it to remember what you've heard, what stuck, and what you'd recommend to someone else.

---

## Scope

- **Albums** — full-lengths, EPs
- **Singles** — only if worth noting
- **Out of scope** — playlists, passive background listening

---

## How It Works

1. After a focused listen: use the **Album Review** template (`templates/Album Review.md`) to capture your thoughts
2. File the review as `domains/music/[artist-slug]-[album-slug].md` (e.g. `radiohead-ok-computer.md`)
3. Add a row to `index.md` with your rating and a one-liner

---

## Conventions

- **Rating:** 1–5
- **File naming:** `[artist]-[album].md` — lowercase, hyphen-separated
- Reviews are conversational Q&A, not formal criticism
