# Entertainment Domain

> Tracks movies and shows you want to watch. Managed conversationally through your Second Brain.
> Last updated: YYYY-MM-DD

---

## Purpose

A friction-free capture layer for entertainment. When you see something in passing — a trailer, a recommendation, a poster — tell your Second Brain and it gets logged. No app to open, no list to forget.

---

## Scope

- **Movies** — theatrical, streaming, on-demand
- **Shows** — series, limited series, docuseries
- **Out of scope** — video games (add a separate domain if needed)

---

## How It Works

1. Mention a title in conversation → add it to `BACKLOG.md`
2. Every entry gets: genre, release year, a short description, and a why-you-want-it note
3. Entries age off to `WATCHED.md` when you've seen them, with a personal rating

---

## Conventions

- **Status tags:** `want` · `watching` · `watched` · `dropped`
- **Priority:** `high` · `normal` · `low`
- **Type:** `film` · `series` · `limited`
