# Health Domain

> Track your health and fitness goals, log progress, and hold yourself accountable month to month.
> Last updated: YYYY-MM-DD

---

## Purpose

A private log for health goals and progress. Not a replacement for medical advice — just a place to keep targets visible and progress honest.

---

## Scope

- Weight and body composition goals
- Fitness habits and exercise cadence
- Dietary guidelines or restrictions you want to track
- Monthly progress log

---

## How It Works

1. Set your goals in `GOALS.md` — be specific about targets and timelines
2. Log progress monthly in `vitals-log.md`
3. Review `GOALS.md` monthly and adjust targets as needed
